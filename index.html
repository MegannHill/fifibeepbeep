What I offer

Lessons for growing performers.

Whether you are preparing for a school musical, building your book, or learning how your voice works, lessons are designed around your goals and your current stage of development.

Voice Lessons

Build vocal confidence through breath, resonance, registration, range, agility, and healthy tone production.

Audition Coaching

Choose strong audition cuts, clarify character choices, prepare accompaniment tracks, and practice the full room-entry experience.

Musical Theatre Coaching

Connect technique to storytelling so songs feel specific, expressive, and performance-ready.

Meet Megann

Teacher, theatre lifer, and joyful voice nerd.

Hi, I’m Megann. My work centers on helping singers understand their voices, trust their instincts, and bring more truth and specificity to the music they perform.

I especially love working with musical theatre performers, young singers, and students preparing for auditions. My lessons are warm, direct, creative, and tailored to the person in front of me.

Studio approach

Technique that supports the story.

The goal is not just to sound good. The goal is to understand what your voice can do, make brave choices, and communicate with clarity.

Healthy Technique

  • Breath coordination
  • Registration and passaggio work
  • Belt, mix, and legit style
  • Warmups tailored to each singer

Musicianship

  • Interval ear training
  • Rhythm and phrasing
  • Memorization strategies
  • Working with accompaniment tracks

Performance

  • Character-driven choices
  • Text and lyric work
  • Audition confidence
  • Specific, repeatable performance beats
